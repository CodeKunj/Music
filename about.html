About Kunj Rana

Crafting Sonic Excellence

I'm Kunj Rana, a professional Music Producer and Mixing & Mastering Engineer based in India. My journey with audio began when I was fascinated by how the same song could sound completely different across different systems — and I made it my mission to understand why.

Over the past 5+ years, I've had the privilege of working with independent artists, YouTube creators, Bollywood composers, and podcasters across genres ranging from LoFi hip hop to high-energy trap and soulful Bollywood arrangements.

My philosophy is simple: every project deserves the same level of care and attention regardless of budget. I believe great sound is accessible, and I work tirelessly to ensure every track I touch sounds its absolute best.
